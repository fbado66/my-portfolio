About Me

I'm a Software Engineer.

Focused Full Stack Engineer with a background in Applied Mathematics specializing in user experience. I enjoy learning the functionality of things, how users interact with technology every day, and finding ways to push the limits of my knowledge to come up with lateral thinking on enhancing applications. And so my inspiration to become a web developer was born from having an idea and finding ways to make it into a web application with a clean user interface.

I have 6+ years in the service industry, learning first-handed how to improve user's experience from working with Customer Relationship Management Platforms (CRM), dealing with sub-contractors, all the way to customer services.

Outside of work, you can find me watching DIY videos, and attempting to recreate carpentry work, enjoying a good chess game, or helping a family member with Math homework.
